ingredients:
1 cup butter (230g)
2 cups sugar
4 eggs
3 cups flour
4 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup milk
1 tsp vanilla

1. mix butter and sugar, then eggs.
2. sift together dry ingredients, then add to butter/egg mixture in stages, alternating with milk.
3. butter pans and place circle of wax paper in bottoms.
4. pour mix into pans, level it out, and place in preheated oven.
5. bake 45 minutes at 180 C. or 350 F. with fan.
6. allow to cool for 5 minutes, then turn out on racks to cool thoroughly. Remove wax paper.

From James Beard
